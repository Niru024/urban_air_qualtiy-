Frequently Asked Questions

What is AQI? expand_more

AQI (Air Quality Index) indicates how polluted the air is and what health effects might be experienced by the public.

How often is air quality data updated? expand_more

Our system updates air quality data every 15 minutes using live sensor networks and satellite data.

Which pollutants are monitored? expand_more

We monitor major pollutants including PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO₂, SO₂, and O₃ to provide a complete AQI score.

Is Urban AQI free to use? expand_more

Yes, basic AQI information is completely free. Advanced features like alerts and analytics may require an account.

How accurate is the AQI data? expand_more

Our data is sourced from certified government sensors, satellite imagery, and AI-based validation models for high accuracy.

Can developers use Urban AQI data? expand_more

Yes, we provide an API for developers, researchers, and organizations. API access details are available in the API section.
